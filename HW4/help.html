QUIZ MAKER HELP

by Team Awesome

CHANGING THE QUIZ NAME

Enter the quiz title in the box next to the total points, above the drag area.

ADDING QUESTIONS

Double click on the question type, or select the question and drag to the area indictaed on the right side of the page.

DELETING QUESTIONS

Click the trash can next to the question you'd like to delete.

REARRANGING QUESTIONS

Click the question you would like to move and drag up or down to the desired location. You can also move the question immediately to the top of the list by clicking the up arrow, or to the bottom of the list by clicking the down arrow.

CHANGING POINT VALUES

Click the point value in the question you would like to edit and enter a new value. The points value next to the quiz title will change to reflect the total number of points in the quiz.

INDICATING CORRECT ANSWERS

Multiple Choice Select the checkboxes next to the correct answers.
True or False Select the button next to the correct answer.
Fill-in the Blank Type the sentence out as normal, and enclose the answer in square brackets, '[' and ']'. For example:
Fill-in After Enter the correct answer in the second box. Add additional answers as needed.
Short Answer Enter the correct answer in the second box.

ADDING ANSWERS

Multiple choice and fill-in after questions:
Click the button with the '+' symbol on it to add a new answer.

REMOVING ANSWERS

Multiple choice and fill-in after questions:
Click the button with the '-' symbol on it to remove an answer.
